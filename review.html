    • THIS WEEK'S READERS' BEST!!

    "POWERLESS" by Lauren Roberts

    Welcome to the addictive, heart-pounding universe of Powerless. This masterpiece throws you into a world of lethal magic and deadly trials, where a sizzling, forbidden romance unfolds under the threat of execution. This isn't just a fantasy; it's your next five star read waiting to devour your weekend.

    Powerless is the first book in a trilogy a Young Adult Dystopian Romantasy where the romantic plot is central to the journey.

    "ONCE UPON A BROKEN HEART" by Stephanie Garber

    Once Upon a Broken Heart is the enchanting first book in a bestselling series by Stephanie Garber that serves as a sequel trilogy to the beloved Caraval universe. If you're craving a story that feels like a classic fairytale spun with dark magic, wicked twists, and a irresistible dose of romance, this is your next must-read.

    "A LOVE LETTER TO WHISKEY" by Kandi Steiner

    Forget sweet romances this is a story that captures the raw, beautiful, and destructive power of an obsession that feels more like a fate than a choice. Love Letters to Whiskey is a contemporary romance that has left a trail of captivated, heart in their throats readers in its wake.

    "THE HOUSEMAID" by Freida McFadden

    You've been searching for a fresh start. You've answered an ad for a live in housemaid. The salary is generous, the brownstone is beautiful, and the Winchester family seems… perfect. But in this house, you'll soon learn that every family has secrets. And some secrets are deadly.

    Welcome to the twisted, unputdownable world of The Housemaid, a #1 bestselling psychological thriller that will have you reading late into the night, questioning every character and trusting no one.
